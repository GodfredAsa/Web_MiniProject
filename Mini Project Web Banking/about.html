History of MBank

People, Technology, and Customer Service  MBank Plc was established in May 1990 and began operations as a commercial bank in July of that year.  Following a highly profitable Initial Public Offering, the bank became a public limited company on June 17, 2004 and was listed on the Ghana Stock Exchange (GSE) on October 21, 2004. (IPO). MBank  currently has approximately one million shareholders and is Ghana's largest bank by tier-1 stock. In 2013, the bank listed $850 million in shares on the London Stock Exchange at $6.80 apiece (LSE).

Our Business

The banking and financial services industry in Ghana has seen substantial improvements in the quality of service delivery and the availability of superior financial products and packages over the last two decades, and MBank has gained a well-deserved reputation as a customer-oriented, business-friendly, and socially relevant bank.

Vision Statement

We aspire to be a leading, technology-driven global financial institution that offers a distinctively distinct range of financial services. The bank's overarching goal is to establish the MBank brand as a reputable international financial services network known for innovation, superior customer service, and performance, all while creating premium value for all stakeholders.

Mision Statement

MBank (Ghana) Limited seeks to distinguish itself in the banking industry through service quality, a commitment to providing a one-of-a-kind customer experience, and a diverse client base.
